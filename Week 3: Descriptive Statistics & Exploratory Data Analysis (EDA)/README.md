Mall Customer Segmentation Dataset

This dataset contains demographic and behavioral information about customers visiting a shopping mall. It is commonly used in marketing analytics and data science courses to explore customer behavior and perform customer segmentation.

The dataset includes information on 200 customers and provides basic attributes that help analyze spending patterns and demographic characteristics.

Variables in the Dataset
Variable	Description
CustomerID	Unique identifier assigned to each customer
Gender	Gender of the customer (Male / Female)
Age	Age of the customer
Annual Income (k$)	Annual income of the customer in thousands of dollars
Spending Score (1–100)	A score assigned by the mall based on customer purchasing behavior

The Spending Score represents how actively a customer shops at the mall. Higher values indicate customers who tend to spend more frequently or purchase higher-value items.

Purpose of the Dataset

This dataset is designed to help analysts explore customer characteristics and identify patterns in purchasing behavior. Typical analytical tasks include:

Descriptive statistics

Exploratory data analysis (EDA)

Customer segmentation

Clustering analysis

Visualization of customer spending patterns

For example, analysts may explore how age, income, and gender relate to spending behavior, or identify groups of customers with similar characteristics for targeted marketing strategies.
